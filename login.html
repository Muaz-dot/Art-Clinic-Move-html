About Us

Iftu Clinic, a place where healing and education converge. Iftu Clinic is a vital healthcare facility nestled within the vibrant of Dire Dawa City, Ethiopia. As part of the esteemed Dire dawa city, this clinic serves as a beacon of care and compassion for the patients. Our mission extends beyond medical treatment. We are committed to nurturing the next generation of healthcare professionals. Guided by experienced professor. Nestled amidst the lush hills of Dire Dawa, the Iftu Clinic stands as a beacon of healing and wisdom.
